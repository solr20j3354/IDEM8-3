Actividad 4: Vocabulario de síntomas y enfermedades estudiarlo practicando la lectura con ayuda del profesor.

-1 AN ALLERGY: A negative reaction to something you eat or touch
Una reacción negativa a algo que comes o tocas
Problema: she has an allergy
Sugerencia: She should go to the pharmacy and buy a nasal spray

-2 ASTHMA: Respiratory problem, difficulty of breathing
Problema respiratorio o dificultad para respirar
Problema: she has asthma
Sugerencia: How about you take one inhalation of a pain reliever or rescue inhaler every 30 to 60 seconds, with a maximum of 10 inhalations

-3 A BACKACHE: A prolonged pain in the back
Un dolor prolongado en la espalda
Problema: Santiago have a backache
Sugerencia: Santiago should go to the doctor to specify what the back pain is due to and that it is not something serious

-4 A BROKEN LEG: A bone in the leg is broken
Se rompe un hueso de la pierna
Problema: My cousin has a broken
Sugerencia: Why don´t you go to the doctor to get a lesson and do an x-ray?

-5 A COUGH: The act of expelling air from the lungs with a sudden sharp sound.
El acto de expulsar el aire de los pulmones con un sonido agudo repentino
Problema: They have a cough
Sugerencia: They should take defrost to get better

-6 A COLD: A common viral infection which causes mucus to run from the nose, gives a sore throat and often includes
Una infección viral común que hace que la mucosidad salga de la nariz, da dolor de garganta y a menudo incluye estornudos
Problema: Susanita has a cold
Sugerencia: Susanita should go to sleep and no got out in rainy weather, she should also drink hot drinks with ginger

-7 AN EARACHE: Pain inside the ear
Dolor de oído
Problema: María has an earache
Sugerencia: She should chew sugar les gum to unclog the ear

-8 A FEVER: An abnormally high body temperature, usually accompanied by shivering and a headache
Temperatura corporal anormalmente alta generalmente acompañada de escalofríos y dolor generalmente acompañada de escalofríos y dolor de cabeza
Problema: My niece has a fever
Sugerencia: She should drink a lot of liquid and rest

-9 THE FLU: Is the common name give for influenza. It is a contagious viral infection of the respiratory passages that causes fever and sever aching
Si el nombre común vive parta influenza.es una infección viral contagiosa de las vías respiratorias que causa fiebre y dolor severo
Problema: My cat has the flu
Sugerencia: How about going to the vet to see what they give you for the flu

-10 A HEADACHE: A continuos pain in the head
Un dolor continuo en la cabeza
Problema: Erika has a headache
Sugerencia: Erika should go to the pharmacy and buy a box of sevedol

-11 HEATBURN: A form of indigestion felt as a burning sensation in the chest.
Una forma de indigestión que se siente como una sensación de ardor en el pecho
Problema: A boy have a heartburn
Sugerencia: Why doesn´t the child reduces fatty foods and anything else that triggers symptoms and drink to sleep in a more upring position

-12 A RASH: A lot of small read spots and the skin that are usually itchy.
Muchas manchas en la piel que suelen picar
Problema: My cousin Sara has a rash
Sugerencia: Sara should go to the doctor so that he can recommend a cream, but in the meantime, she can apply betamethasone or hydrocortisone where she has the rash

-13 A SORE THROATH: A condition of pain in the throat, typically caused by inflammation of it
Dolor de garganta, normalmente causado por una inflamación de la misma
Problema: My father has a sore throat
Sugerencia: How about a hot aguapanela with lemon and ginger?

-14 A SPRAIN: An injury to a joint in your body, especially your wrist or ankle caused by suddenly twisting it
Lesión en una articulación del cuerpo especialmente en la muñeca o el tobillo, causado por una torción repentina
Problema: Paramedic has a sprain
Sugerencia: The paramedic should apply acetal reduce swelling and pain in the ankle

-15 A STOMACHACHE: The pain in a person´s belly
El dolor en el vientre de una persona
Problema: My sister has a stomachache
Sugerencia: My sister should avoid lying down and should take a bath with lukewarm water or use a heating bag

-16 A toothache: The pain in a tooth on teeth
El dolor de muelas o dientes
Problema: My neighbor Samuel has a toothache
Sugerencia: He should put ice on his tooth and take acetaminophen or ibuprofen
